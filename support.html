FAQ

Why is my score low?

DRIPSCAN rates based on color coordination, fit, cohesion, and how on-trend your outfit is. Plain or basic outfits score lower. Try adding layers, accessories, or better color matching.

Why can't I scan anymore?

Free users get 5 scans. After that, upgrade to DRIPSCAN Pro for unlimited scans, full score breakdowns, and outfit history.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app, tap the scan button to trigger the paywall, then tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom.
